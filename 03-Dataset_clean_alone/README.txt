### Traits units ###

label: species name
TLDiet: trophic level
Temperature: temperature in degree celcius 

habitatbenthopelagic: binary: species habitat is benthopelagic : yes/no
habitatdemersal: binary: species habitat is demersal: yes/no
habitatpelagic: binary: species habitat is demersal: yes/no

c_m: log-transformed resting metabolic rate (RMR)
tm: log-transformed maturation age
Lm: log-transformed maturation length
tmax: log-transformed maximum age
K: log-transformed growth coefficient
Woo: log-transformed infinite weight
M: log-transformed mortality rate
Loo: log-transformed infinite length 
fecundity: log-transformed relative fecundity
Min_caudalpeduncle_depth: log-transformed minimum caudal peduncle depth
Lower_jaw_length: log-transformed lower jaw length
Max_body_depth: log-transformed maximum body depth
Max_body_width: log-transformed maximum body width
